Supply Diversification
Strategic Pivot: China & Turkey
Interim Strategy
Business Continuity During Recovery
As an alternative to Brazil, sourcing poultry from China and Turkey is a strategic interim option for the UAE market. This approach ensures business continuity during the post-war recovery period, allowing operations to proceed without disruption while Brazil bookings and arrivals are being finalised. By diversifying our supply sources, we can keep products in stock, stabilise sales, and protect profit margins.

Risk Mitigation
Operational Reserve Fund

To ensure consistent operational stability and strengthen our market presence, it is essential to maintain a minimum reserve equivalent to 2 FCL, approximately $250,000 USD, as a backup fund.

This reserve enables us to sustain day-to-day operations without relying on additional capital or diverting funds from core resources. With this financial buffer, our team can stay flexible, respond quickly to market needs, and operate with confidence.

Moreover, maintaining such a reserve positions us as a reliable and strong supplier, enhancing our visibility and credibility in the market while reinforcing trust with partners and clients.
